◆ distribution_type

enum class distribution_type
strong

Enumeration of probability distribution types supported in SedSat3.

Defines the statistical distributions available for modeling uncertainty in tracer concentrations, parameter priors, and observational errors.

Enumerator
normal 

Normal (Gaussian) distribution: p(x) = N(μ, σ²)

lognormal 

Lognormal distribution: ln(x) ~ N(μ, σ²), for strictly positive variables.

dirichlet 

Dirichlet distribution (treated as uniform on simplex in current implementation)

none 

No distribution assigned (uninitialized state)

uniform 

Uniform distribution on [0,1].

enum class parameter_mode
strong

Specifies whether to use direct data statistics or fitted distribution parameters.

Controls how mean/variance are calculated from a Distribution object:

  • direct: Use empirical statistics from original data (mean_val, std_val)
  • based_on_fitted_distribution: Calculate from distribution parameters
Enumerator
direct 

Use empirical statistics from data.

based_on_fitted_distribution
